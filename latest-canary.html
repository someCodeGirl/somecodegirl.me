What is a warrant canary?

A warrant canary works as a sort of digital "canary in a coal mine". There are legal requests for information that can include gag orders. These requests could put the privacy and safety of users of my site or other projects at risk. Therefore, I publish a quarterly canary to state that I have not received any such requests. If the canary ever fails to update or the claim ever changes, then one could assume I have received a request and am restricted from saying as much by a gag order.

For more information about canaries, check out EFF's page about them.

You don't collect user data so why does it matter?

Technically it doesn't, but in case a project or service of mine does start to collect user data for any reason (think user account on a game or website, communications submitted through this site, etc) then I am already prepared and have a history for you to look through and audit.
